Picker

Picker helps you decide where you and your friends should go get food!

How To Play
When each friend joins the room, they are given a certain amount of "HungerBucks". The friend will then spend their HungerBucks as they please on the restaurants of their desire. When the room creator closes the room, each friend will see which restaurants nabbed the most HungerBucks. The restaurant with the most HungerBucks is where you eat.

The room entered does not exist.
